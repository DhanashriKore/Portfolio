This project focuses on predicting car prices using machine learning techniques. It leverages a dataset that includes various car attributes such as make, model, year, mileage, engine size, and fuel type to estimate the market price of vehicles. Several machine learning algorithms were implemented, including Linear Regression, Decision Trees, Random Forest, and Gradient Boosting, to determine the most accurate model for price prediction. The models were assessed using metrics like Mean Squared Error (MSE) and R-squared to ensure their effectiveness. Through feature engineering and hyperparameter tuning, the project optimized the models to improve predictive accuracy, offering valuable insights for car buyers and sellers in determining fair market value.
